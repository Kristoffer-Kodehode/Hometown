Visit Bergen!

Enjoy the culture and sights of the rainy city of Norway

Things you can do and see:

See the city from above and enjoy nature at Mt. Fløyen, or any of the other mountains surrounding the city!

Remember to bring good shoes!

Enjoy the sounds of the drums of one of Bergens old traditional Buekorps!

Some locals may have their beef with loud drumming some afternoons during the spring season, but this traditional youth activity has a long history!

Or simply enjoy the rain!

The rains of Bergen are widely recognized for their longevity and nigh-on ever-present nature!

Umbrellas not required, nor neccessary!
